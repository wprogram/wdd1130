History

The Company was created to Provide enjoyment and sports engagement. The aim was to create a platform allowing people to explore the world of sports, meet like-minded people, and have fun playing the sport they love. Enjoyment with family and friends was the primary goal of creating the platform. People can create their own teams and connect with others to play the sports they want, making meeting people with similar interests easier. The platform also lets people stay informed about the latest news and updates in the world of sports.
